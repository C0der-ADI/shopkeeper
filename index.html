The Data Core

Offline-First Reliability

Because shopkeepers can't always rely on data or Wi-Fi.

Offline Database

Powered by local Room database technology. All your inventory, sales, and history stored securely on-device—no internet required for daily operations.

Dual-Pricing Engine

Separate data slots for Piece and Bundle (Wholesale) pricing. Sell single items or in bulk with automatic price calculation.

Automated Logs

A built-in history ledger that automatically records every stock change, sale, and transaction. Never lose track of what happened when.
